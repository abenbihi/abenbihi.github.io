Bio

I am interested in localisation and mapping and how to develop solutions that work in the real world. Some of the challenges I work on include large-scale mapping, long-term localisation, localisation in natural environments, localisation across extreme change of variations, and privacy-preserving localisation. Feel free to reach out if you want to chat about these topics.

Previously, I interned at Niantic, worked at the Visual Recognition Group, and received my PhD from CentraleSupelec at the DREAM Lab, under the supervision of Cédric Pradalier and Matthieu Geist, tackling long-term localisation problems across seasons in challenging outdoor environments.
