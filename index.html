Bio

I am a 3rd-year PhD student at the DREAM Lab, under the supervision of Cédric Pradalier and Matthieu Geist, where I bend stubborn computers to my will to solve any exciting problems. Currently, these problems regard long-term localisation across seasons in challenging outdoor environments with strong seasonal and weather variations. I also develop automatic visual inspection software for electronic industry.

I am looking for a postdoc or an industrial research position related to outdoor perception in challenging environments.
