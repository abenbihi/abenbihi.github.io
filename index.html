Bio

I am a 3rd-year PhD student at the DREAM Lab, under the supervision of Cédric Pradalier and Matthieu Geist, where I bend stubborn computers to my will to solve any exciting problems. Currently, these problems regard computer vision and machine learning. I also develop automatic visual inspection software for electronic industry.

I am looking for a postdoc or other positions that involve research, ML and software.
